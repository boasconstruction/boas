What we do

INTERIOR DESIGN AND DECORATING

We believe that every client has unique requirements and taste. First and foremost, we listen to our clients' needs very carefully. And for those clients who want to be involved in the design process, we are more than happy to collaborate with you during the entire creating process. Or we can also provide a full design and decorating services including design plans, purchasing, installation, and decorating based on our consultation.

NEW CONSTRUCTION OR RENOVATION PLANNING

Our services range from residential remodels through new construction. From our complimentary in-home consultation through the finishing touches, our design and construction teams are committed to ensuring quality craftsmanship, design and value. We will provide all the necessary plans, drawings and schedules for the construction phase of your project, giving you everything your team of professionals will need to execute.

KITCHEN OR BATHROOM RENOVATION PLANNING

We can create a kitchen or bathroom renovation plan that is just right for you and your budget. We have chosen the best suppliers and manufactures of granite, custom cabinets, windows, doors, lumber, lighting, flooring, and all materials for your home building or remodel project. Our clients have access to our vendors‘ showrooms to select which materials will be used for their projects.
